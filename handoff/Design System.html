Broomva Design System v1

Calm, monochrome, on a blue axis

The primitives designed for Mission Control, Broomva's work plane: the work-as-noun model, the plain-voice state vocabulary, the disclosure ladder, and the Undertow live signal. Every black is a barely-perceptible dark blue, and depth cues carry frosted liquid-glass blue. It reads as monochrome until you look. Then everything is faintly, coolly blue.

Work is the noun The gate is yours The Undertow: presence, not progress Glass is earned, not ambient Sentence case everywhere

Why

autonomy is the moat · control is the engine
Broomva orchestrates loops of agentic workflows under structure: control, governance, and bio-inspired principles of metacognition, persistence, stability and flow. The scarce resource is unsupervised hours — how long an agent runs before a human must look. The design language makes that legible and calm.
The workspace is the substrateFS + sh. A folder is work at any scale; frontmatter is the contract. Sessions are the verb — work is the noun.
Chat is a projectionA session renders the work; it never owns it. The same run appears in a panel, a thread, a handoff page.
The orchestrator is an agentA session that schedules sessions: presence in the chrome, a wake log, schedules set in a sentence — never a settings page.
The tick is a promptEvery wake has a cause: a worker returning, your message, an interval, a self-set routine. The loop is legible.
The branch is the receiptEvidence over claims: run branches, diffstats, judge verdicts. Never fake progress percentages.
The gate is yoursNo loop can auto-Done. The look compresses hours to 90 seconds: what changed · what it decided · what it asks.

Color

monochrome on a cool axis · one hue family · color only carries signal
Ink and cool grays. Every neutral sits at hue 265 with whisper chroma. The ink replaces #0d0d0d.

Accents and frost. ai-blue owns focus, hover frost and glow; accent-blue appears only when two accents must coexist.

Depth

two shadow tiers, all faintly blue · glass in exactly three places
Shadows. Edge for cards at rest; the composer halo is the one dramatic cue; glow marks active surfaces.
edge card-hover composer halo glow
Glass. Only overlays, popovers and the composer. The inner light line is what makes it glass. Cards and chrome stay matte.

Voice

plain language · second person · lead with the verb

Do

✓"Quick check first: are you on Google or Microsoft?"
