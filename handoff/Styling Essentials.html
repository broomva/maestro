07

What must survive

  • The ink is blue-black, not black, and every gray sits on the cool axis (hue 265). This alone carries most of the identity.
  • Color earns its place — status, the ai-blue interaction layer, live-signal glow. No gradient washes, no colored accent strips.
  • Glass is earned by overlays, popovers and the composer, each with an inner light line. Everything else is matte.
  • Every shadow carries a hint of blue, in three restrained tiers. The composer halo is the only dramatic depth cue.
  • Sentence case, plain voice, calm motion. No uppercase eyebrows, no bouncy entrances, no em dashes in chrome.
