Three rungs. The product must be fully operable from the first two; the third exists to prove they were telling the truth.
1 · Ambient — always onPlain-language feed, attention chip, bench, Undertow. Answers "what needs me, what's moving." No substrate words on this layer.the feed · the chrome
2 · The gate — on demandThe look: what changed · what it decided · what it asks. Control is verbs, not configuration — approve, send back, grant, point.the look · the asks
3 · The receipts — opt-inInspector tabs: frontmatter chips, scope badges, run history, branches. For verifying trust, never required for operating.inspector · activity · wake log
The translation table. Every substrate concept maps to exactly one surface primitive — if a feature needs the user to learn a substrate word, the translation has failed.
